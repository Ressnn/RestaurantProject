Chick-Fil-A

Chick-fil-A is the largest chicken, and the third largest American fast food restaurant chain, whose specialty is chicken sandwiches. Their headquarters are in College Park, Georgia and they were founded in May 1946.

Panda Express

Panda Express is a fast food restaurant chain which serves American Chinese cuisine. With approximately 2,000+ locations, it is the largest Asian segment restaurant chain in the United States, where it was founded and is mainly located.

Jeni's Ice Cream

Jeni's Splendid Ice Creams is an artisan ice cream company headquartered in Columbus, Ohio, with national distribution. Jeni's is known for its creative flavors and its high-quality ingredients, has been featured nationally in the press as an artisan ice cream maker, and has won two Sofi Awards.

Twisted Taco

From a single Midtown Atlanta restaurant to locations throughout Atlanta and an ever growing national presence on college campuses, the emphasis at Twisted Taco has always been on good food and good times. Twisted Taco’s mantra is to provide the freshest ingredients, sourced locally when possible, and be a leader in living and working “green,” as evidenced by our attention to sustainable products such as our compostable “to go” boxes.
